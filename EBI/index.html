You Are Welcome

At Eden Bulb, we believe that education is a divine calling–a sacred partnership between home, school, and heaven. Our mission is to raise children who are spiritually grounded, intellectually sound, and morally upright, through the guidance of the Holy Spirit, we combine strong academics with steadfast Christian values to nurture excellence in character and learning.

Every child at Eden Bulb is a seed of greatness planted by God, and it is our privilege to water those seeds with love, discipline, and knowledge until they shine forth as global heights.

Thank you for trusting us with your child's growth. Together, we will continue to build a generation that stands out–academically, spiritually and morally.

With Love,
Pastor (Mrs) Queenette Ulu
Proprietress, Eden Bulb International School.
